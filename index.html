About the Game

One Game. Four Adventures.

Horse Riding & Cart Carriage is a 3D adventure game built for true horse lovers. Ride majestic horses, drive traditional carts and carriages, jump over thrilling obstacle courses, and master horseback archery — all in one immersive experience across beautiful forest environments.

New to the game? Easy-to-learn controls let every player jump straight into the saddle — no riding experience needed. Master the reins, the jumps, and the bow at your own pace.

Game Modes

Four Ways to Ride

🐎

Horse Riding

Ride majestic horses through scenic forests, countryside trails, and exciting adventure routes. Improve your riding skills while completing engaging missions.

🛒

Cart Carriage Driving

Drive traditional horse-drawn carts and carriages across beautiful landscapes. Navigate challenging paths, transport cargo, and master realistic carriage controls.

🏇

Horse Jumping

Challenge yourself with exciting obstacle courses. Jump over fences, wooden barriers, rocks, and other obstacles while maintaining speed and perfect timing.

🏹

Horseback Archery

Become a skilled horseback archer by riding through the wilderness and completing exciting archery missions. Test your aim and improve your accuracy.

Unlock & Customize

Build Your Stable

Progress through missions to unlock new horses, gear, and equipment for your adventure.

🐴

Horse Breeds & Skins

Unlock multiple beautiful horse breeds along with different skins and colors to make your stable your own.

🛞

Carts & Carriages

Collect traditional carts and carriages, each with their own look and handling for your driving adventures.

🏹

Outfits & Bows

Equip stylish rider outfits and powerful bows and arrows, then upgrade your gear as you progress.
